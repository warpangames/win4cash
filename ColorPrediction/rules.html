Game Overview

The Color Prediction Game allows you to place bets in three different categories:

  • Bet on Color
  • Bet on Number
  • Bet on Big or Small

Bet on Color

Colors Available: Red, Blue, Green

Payout: 2x your bet amount

How to Play:

  1. Choose one of the three colors: Red, Blue, or Green.
  2. Place your bet on the selected color.
  3. If the chosen color is selected during the draw, you win 2 times the amount you bet.

Bet on Big or Small

Small Numbers: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4

Big Numbers: 5, 6, 7, 8, 9

Payout: 1.5x your bet amount

How to Play:

  1. Choose either "Big" (numbers 5-9) or "Small" (numbers 0-4).
  2. Place your bet on the selected category.
  3. If a number from the chosen category is drawn, you win 1.5 times the amount you bet.

General Rules

  • Bet Placement: Players must place their bets before the draw.
  • Draw: A number and color are randomly selected during each draw.
  • Winning: Payouts are based on the type of bet placed and the drawn result.
  • Multiple Bets: Players can place bets on multiple categories in a single round.

Example

If you place a bet of $10 on Red, and Red is drawn, you win $20.

If you place a bet of $10 on the number 0, and 0 is drawn, you win $90.

If you place a bet of $10 on Big, and a number between 5 and 9 is drawn, you win $15.

Additional Information

  • Bets must be placed before the draw starts.
  • The game ensures fairness and randomness in the draw process.
  • Players can check their betting history and current balance in their account section.
